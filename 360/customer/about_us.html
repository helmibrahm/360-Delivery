Who we are...

We are more than just a delivery service—we are a team of dedicated students committed to enhancing campus convenience. Our journey began out of necessity, sparked by the challenges faced by students like you. The idea took root as we recognized the hurdles of navigating to HEPA for parcel pick-ups. The Genesis of 360 Delivery is a testament to the innovation and determination that birthed this solution for our campus community. Get to know the driving force behind 360 Delivery, where our founding story fuels our passion for redefining campus deliveries.

Introduction

Your Campus Convenience Partner

Welcome to 360 Delivery, your go-to solution for hassle-free parcel deliveries on campus. Designed with students in mind, our service is tailored to provide a convenient alternative for those navigating the distance to HEPA, ensuring you get your orders without the hassle.

Service Overview

Simplified Campus Deliveries

At 360 Delivery, we've reimagined parcel services for students. We specialize in collecting your orders from HEPA, the designated drop point, and delivering them right to your doorstep. No need to worry about long walks or transportation hurdles – we've got you covered.

How It Works ?

Effortless Ordering and Delivery

Ordering with 360 Delivery is as easy as 1-2-3. Simply place your order at HEPA, and we'll take care of the rest. Our team will collect your items and ensure a prompt and secure delivery to your location on campus. It's that simple!
